About Me

Born in 1996 and raised in North-East Ohio from day one. From a young age, I developed an affinity for creation whether it be playing music, drawing, cooking, and everything in between. In addition to that, I've always had a love of video gaming. Naturally, these interests led me to want to create my own games. This desire led me to the discovery of the We Can Code IT Academy in Cleveland, Ohio, where new doors opened me up to the world of software development, both inside and outside the world of gaming. Equipped with the skills needed to be a proactive and productive member of any develpment team, I'm now pursuing the goal of getting my foot in the door within the develpoment community. Whether it's website design, console applications, or video games, my passion for creation is as alive and strong now as it has ever been!
